Design Principles are a tool for creating better and more consistent designs and experiences. They are high level rules that guide detailed design decisions. They are often written down within bigger style guides, pattern libraries or design systems. Know a resource that isn't listed below? Feel free to create a new pull request, or open an issue. Please read the contribution guidelines before contributing.
