Geocaching is an outdoor recreational activity, in which participants use a Global Positioning System receiver or mobile device and other navigational techniques to hide and seek containers, called geocaches or caches, at specific locations marked by coordinates all over the world. Contributions welcome! Read the contribution guidelines first.  Leave a star if you liked this list! Contents Websites Apps Other Websites Geocaching.com - Official international website. Shop Geocaching - Official geocaching shop where you can buy cache containers, trackables, wearables and more. Project-GC - Website that gives a lot of statistics about your geocaching account. GeoCheck - Coordinates checker for geocaches. Certitude - Tool used to validate solutions for geocaching puzzles. Geocaching.su - Website for geocaching in the post-Soviet countries. Geocaching Toolbox - A lot of tools for geocachers mainly to solve puzzles and do operations with coordinates. My Geocaching Profile - Website to build a detailed profile of your geocaching accomplishments. CacheSleuth - Website with many useful tools for geocaching (mainly text decoders). LonelyCache - Website with the list of geocaches that were not found in many years. GC Wizard Web View - A web view of the GC Wizard app. SolvedJigidi - The database of solved Jigidi geocaches. Webwigo - Website for virtual playing of Wherigo geocaches. Apps Geocaching - An official app for geocaching. Wherigo - [iOS] An official app to play Wherigo geocaches. WhereYouGo - [Android] Open source, unofficial app for playing Whereigo geocaches. c:geo - [Android] The most popular free unofficial app for geocaching. Has many tools the official app doesn't have. Cachly - [iOS] Paid unofficial app for geocaching. Has many tools the official app doesn't have. GC Wizard - An open-source tool collection for Android and iOS. It was created to offer Geocachers an offline tool to support them with in-field mysteries and riddles. Geocaching Buddy - Paid geocaching app that recalculates waypoint formulas based on discovered clues when solving multi-caches. TBScan - An app to discover trackables just by pointing your camera at the code. Raccoon - [iOS] Geocaching app that should help you with multi or mystery caches. Geocaching4Locus - [Android] Locus map add-on which allows you to download and import caches directly from Geocaching.com site. GeoGet - [Windows] Geocache manager, where you can manage your final waypoints, add notes or waypoints to geocache or import/export geocache from/to GeoGet. GSAK (Geocaching Swiss Army Knife) - [Windows] Desktop app for managing geocaches and waypoints. CacheStats - [Windows] Application that displays your geocaching statistics. Caching on Kai - [KaiOS] Geocaching app for KaiOS users. Cacher - [Garmin] Gatmin watch app for geocaching. Other pycaching - Python 3 interface for working with Geocaching.com website. GC little helper II - Powerful tool to enhance and extend the functionality of the geocaching website.
