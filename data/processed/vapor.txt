It allows you to take the language you already know if you ever developed an iOS application and use it in a whole new way, to develop fast, scalable and reliable back-end systems that integrate easily with a wide range of third party services. This is a curated list of: modern libraries that easily integrate with Vapor and follow Vapors philosophy of providing simple, clean yet powerful APIs; well-written tutorials, books, videos and education materials; tools to make your development process simpler and more enjoyable; and more!
